Betting University – Free

1. How Implied Probability Works

The bookmaker builds in “vig” (juice). Our calculator shows you the real no-vig probability so you know when a line is +EV.

2. Bankroll Management (The 1% Rule)

Never risk more than 1–2% of your total bankroll on a single bet. This is how pros survive long losing streaks.

3. What is +EV Betting?

When your estimated win probability is higher than the implied probability from the bookmaker – that’s +EV. Our EV slider helps you spot these instantly.
